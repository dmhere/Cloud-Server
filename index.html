About HDFS Cluster and this Service

Hello World
Welcome to the Simpset,
By this service you can setup a hadoop hdfs cluster in 2 modes either over cloud or locally and then give a dashboard to you to control and watch every activuty of your hdfs cluster.
To start, You just need to provide how much datanodes you want, how much space you want in each datanode, the way you want to proced and that's it. You are good to go.
Enjoy using hdfs.
